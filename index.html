About Me

I am a filmmaker, director of photography, and camera operator with hands-on experience in lighting design, cinematography, and live event production. With a background in theatre and visual arts, I bring a technical, detail-oriented approach to storytelling. I regularly work with a wide range of professional camera, lighting, and rigging equipment, ensuring each production runs smoothly and creatively. Whether designing lighting, operating the camera, or managing complex gear setups, I combine technical precision with a strong visual instinct to deliver polished, cinematic results.
